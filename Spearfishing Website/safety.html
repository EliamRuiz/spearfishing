Here are some spearfishing tips for safety!

Awarance

Always double and triple check gear before getting in the water and always let someone know where you are so if you go missing they can notify autorithies and give them a general direction.Always be aware while diving. Also Aware of surroundings, breath control, level of tiredness, gear readiness, fish behavior, pay attention for unattentive boat drivers and etc.

Buddy System

NEVER, NEVER, NEVER dive alone. Identify a great buddy who knows you and that you are always looking out for one another.

Quick release gear and buoyancy

Gear parts like the weight belt must always have a readily available a quick release system. Always verify your bouyancy in ten feet of water or less where you should float enough that your face can keep out of the water.

Speargun Safety

ALWAYS treat your speargun as if it where loaded. Never (in or out of water) point your speargun at anybody. Never bring a loaded boat into the boat.

Training

Take a freediving class and get CPR certified (the whole dive team) so that in case of an emergency you can respond adequately

Stay Hidrated, well identified

Make sure to drink sufficient water. Also, be well identified with dive flags and floats.
