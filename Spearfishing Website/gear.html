Wetsuit

One of the most important part of teh gear as it protects you from the sun and regulates your body temperature.The thicker, two piece, open cells are warmer and the opposite are colder.

Speargun

It is your weapon underwater, the main tool used to catch fish. There are many diferent types, power bands, rollers, inverters, wooden, carbon, metalic ,pneumatic and etc. The simpler ones are power band rail guns, ther longer the stronger and harder to load. The other types you will learn with time.
